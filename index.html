Rhinoplasty

Friday Functional and Cosmetic Rhinoplasty.

Patient who had bilateral nasal obstruction with a right concha bullosa and left septal deviation that wanted a subtle dorsal hump reduction and slight narrowing and elevation of the tip.

Post operatively patient was extremely pleased that the subtle changes enhanced her aesthetics and the straightening of the septum and removal of the concha bullosa significantly improved her breathing.

At 3 months approximately 95% of swelling has decreased. Swelling continues to decrease even up to one year post operatively.
